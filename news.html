SuperStation One Pre-Orders Open

Retro Remake's SuperStation One, an FPGA-powered PlayStation clone, is now available for pre-order. Priced starting at $149.99, this console features original PlayStation controller and memory card ports, analog output, USB, NFC, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and SNAC support. An optional accessory, the SuperDock, includes a CD/DVD drive, USB ports, and an SSD bay. Pre-orders are currently offered at a discounted rate of $180, with the price expected to rise to $225 post-release.

Dell Discounts 32-Inch 4K Gaming Monito

Dell is offering a significant discount on the Alienware AW3225QF, a 32-inch 4K gaming monitor. Originally priced at $1,200, it is now available for $765. The monitor boasts a slightly curved 4K QD-OLED panel for enhanced black levels and vibrant colors, a 240-Hz refresh rate, and Nvidia G-Sync support for smoother gameplay. It also includes multiple ports, such as DisplayPort 1.4, HDMI 2.1, and a USB 3.2 Gen 1 hub. The discount is valid until February 3, 7 am CST, or while supplies last.
